Lion

Male lions defend the pride’s territory while females do most of the hunting. Despite this, the males eat first. . A lion’s heels don’t touch the ground when it walks.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Habitat: dry savannah forest and deciduous scrub forest
  • Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Average rest time: 21 hours

Lion large, powerfully built cat that is second in size only to the tiger.The proverbial “king of beasts,” the lion has been one of the best-known wild animals since earliest times. Lions are most active at night. An isolated population of about 650 Asiatic lions constitute a slightly smaller race that lives under strict protection in India’s Gir National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary.
